The Scoring Engine

Two Scores. One Answer.

Proprietary. Transparent. Evolving.

The Deal Structure Score
How strong is this deal?
78/100

Before a single lender is evaluated, the deal gets a health rating. LTV, DTI, down payment, FICO tier, credit depth, collections, housing — every factor weighted into one number.

A 92 means most lenders will fight for it. A 54 means structural problems. The Deal Doctor tells you exactly what to change.

Visible to your F&I manager on every deal.
The Lender Match Score
How well does this deal fit each bank?
91/100

Each lender gets its own 0–100 composite. FICO fit, LTV tolerance, DTI threshold, mileage cap, reserve opportunity — all weighted per lender.

OZK scores 91 because LTV and FICO land in their sweet spot. Fifth Third scores 64 because DTI is tighter. The ranking isn't alphabetical — it's mathematical.

Every lender ranked. Highest score = best path.

The Platform

27 Features. Zero Guesswork.

Every tool your F&I manager needs. One screen. One second.

Scoring & Intelligence
01Proprietary Dual Scoring

Every deal gets a Structure Score and every lender gets a Match Score. Both 0–100, 25+ weighted signals. Highest score = best path.

0225+ Bureau Signals Weighted

Not just FICO. Utilization, inquiries, collections, derogs, years in file, housing — each signal shows its exact point impact.

03Three Sort Modes

Max Profit ranks by reserve + F&I gross. Lowest Payment ranks by buy rate. Best Approval ranks by odds. One tap — rankings update instantly.

04Deal Health Monitor

A live 0–100 score visible in the header on every deal. Green = clean. Red = problems. Updates as you change any input.

05Outcome Intelligence System

Six outcomes — Approved, Countered, Declined, Funded, Conditional, Withdrawn. Submit-first flow. 15 seconds per outcome. The engine learns from every one.

Reserve & Rate Tools
06Reserve to the Dollar

Every lender has its own formula — base plus spread, flat percent, tiered flat, spread only. Custom formulas for any dealer agreement. Exact dollar, not estimated.

07Rate Override Slider

Drag the sell rate and watch reserve, payment, total gross, and 'left on table' update live. The exact cost of every concession before you make it.

08Buydown Table

Every 0.25% step from max sell rate to buy rate. Reserve sacrificed, payment reduction, cost-per-dollar of payment saved. Most efficient step highlighted.

09Reverse Payment Calculator

Customer says '$650/mo.' You're at $724. Enter $650 — system shows exactly how much more down at each term. One number in, every answer out.

10Per-Lender Term Control

Each lender card has its own term pills — tap 144, 180, 216, 240 and see that bank's payment update instantly. Global override still available.

Deal Structure & Compliance
11Live DTI · PTI · LTV

Every ratio updates live, color-coded against each lender's thresholds. 48% DTI passes at Medallion but kills BofA — you see it before the bank does.

12Front-End / Backend / Total LTV

Three separate LTV checks per lender — front-end cap (unit only), backend allowance (F&I products as % of invoice), and total combined ceiling.

13F&I Product Intelligence

Load GAP, warranty, service contracts. The system recalculates every lender's backend LTV against their max % of invoice. See which accept your full stack.

14Down Payment Intelligence

Each lender has different minimums by FICO, loan amount, and tier. Shows exactly how short you are per lender and what additional down unlocks each one.

15Mileage Intelligence

BofA caps at 50K gas / 100K diesel. OZK allows 75K / 150K. M&T tops at 40K. Toggle gas or diesel — your entire lender book responds instantly.

16High Credit Exposure Flag

Financing $82K when highest prior credit is $28K? That's 2.9x exposure. The system flags it and shows which lenders care about that metric.

Lender Program Intelligence
17Deal Doctor

No qualifying lenders? Shows the exact move — '$2,100 more down unlocks OZK' or '20 points higher FICO opens Fifth Third.' Dollar amounts, not guesses.

18FICO Gap Badge

Sitting at 738? System shows '2 pts to 740 → 0.25% lower buy rate at OZK.' Per lender, per tier boundary. See what one score bump is worth.

19Acceptance Rules Engine

Toggle BK — 12 lenders disappear, 2 remain. Toggle repo — score caps at 45. Every flag shows who accepts and the conditions.

20Program Notes + Stips

Full notes and stip requirements for every lender, one tap from the result card. Colony requires POI over $100K. Fifth Third needs FFD. No more calling the rep.

21Co-Signer Support

Add a co-borrower with their own FICO. System recalculates approval odds using the stronger borrower where applicable. Shows exact point boost per lender.

22Prior RV / Auto Loan Detection

Some lenders reward prior RV or auto history. Toggle it on and watch those lenders get a score boost. The system knows which banks care.

Desk & Workflow
23FastPass Desk Check

Sales desk needs a quick answer before F&I. Enter FICO, income, sale price, down — get a pre-screen in seconds. Separate tab, simplified view.

24Save / Load / Manage Deals

Every deal saved with full snapshot — structure, results, outcomes, products. Load any deal instantly. History searchable by stock number.

25Deal ID System

Every deal gets a unique ID (DO-2026-0001) visible in the header. Track across your team. Becomes your deal ledger when the backend connects.

26Stock # / VIN Identification

Deals identified by stock number and VIN — not customer name. Stock # auto-fills year, make, model, mileage when inventory feed connects in Pro.

27Gas / Diesel Toggle

One toggle changes every lender's mileage cap simultaneously. BofA goes 50K→100K. OZK goes 75K→150K. Entire lender book recalculates for fuel type.
